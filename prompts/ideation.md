---

Generate 3 innovative micro Web3 service ideas for the Mossland ecosystem.

## Context

Mossland is a blockchain-based metaverse project with:
- **MOC Token**: Mossland's native cryptocurrency
- **Real-world Integration**: Connecting physical locations with virtual spaces
- **NFT Ecosystem**: Digital assets and collectibles
- **AR/VR Experiences**: Immersive technologies

## Requirements

Each idea must:
1. **Benefit the Mossland ecosystem** - Add value to MOC holders or enhance the platform
2. **Be a micro Web3 service** - Small, focused, and achievable
3. **Be implementable** - Realistic scope for autonomous development
4. **Be testable** - Clear success criteria and validation methods

## Selection Criteria

When evaluating ideas, consider:
- **Impact**: Potential benefit to the ecosystem
- **Feasibility**: Technical achievability
- **Innovation**: Novelty and creativity
- **Testability**: Ability to validate success
- **Scope**: Appropriate for micro-service implementation
